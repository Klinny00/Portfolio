About

I'm Karma Lynze, a product designer with skills in coding, interactive design, and graphic design.

My story

I embarked on my journey in UX design 3 years ago. I have a passion for creating user centric designs that create a powerful and positive impact in people's lives. I studied Computer Science, Product Design, and Digital Media in college. With the courses I have taken, I learned how to create functional and aesthetically pleasing designs for users. I had the opportunity to work with 3 startups thus far. My projects consisted of mobile design, rebranding, user research, and web design. In my spare time, I love to create cultural paintings, photographing nature, and sketching!

Current

I am taking courses to expand my knowledge of UX design. I spend most of time learning new mobile developing softwares and experimenting in Figma.
