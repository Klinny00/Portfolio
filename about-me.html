My Story
My Story

My Stacks

I’m a University of Minnesota graduate with a focus on product design, computer science, and digital media, and I’m now pursuing a master’s in human-computer interaction at UCI. Since 2021, I’ve been designing interfaces across mobile, web, AR, VR, and AI, and I’ve also worked on physical products. I’ve had the chance to work at four startups as a UX/UI designer, collaborating with a diverse range of users and focusing on accessible and inclusive design.

Creativity has always been a part of me. Growing up, I fell in love with art and design, and I continue to create modern cultural art on the side. Coming from an underrepresented background, I’m passionate about designing for those who are often left out. I’m excited to keep growing and creating meaningful experiences in this career.
